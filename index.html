HDSci Lab

Health Data Science & Intelligence Laboratory

Led by Dr Tina Wang, the HDSci Lab conducts research at the intersection of healthcare, data science, artificial intelligence, and patient-centred analytics.

Research Focus

  • Health data science for clinical and healthcare decision-making
  • Real-world evidence and patient-centred analytics
  • Statistical and machine learning methods for health data
  • AI-based methods for improved health literacy

Approach

We develop and apply rigorous statistical, data science, and artificial intelligence methods to address real-world healthcare challenges, with a strong emphasis on patient impact, translational relevance, and responsible innovation.

Affiliation

The HDSci Lab is an academic research group led by Dr Tina Wang.
