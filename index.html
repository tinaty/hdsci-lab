HDSci Lab

Health Data Science & Intelligence Laboratory
Led by Dr. Tina Wang

The HDSci Lab conducts research in health data science and intelligence at the intersection of healthcare, data, and patients.

Research Focus

  • Health data science for clinical and healthcare decision-making
  • Real-world evidence and patient-centred analytics
  • Statistical and machine learning methods for health data
  • AI-based methods for improved health literacy

Approach

We develop and apply rigorous statistical, data science, and artificial intelligence methods to address real-world healthcare problems, with a strong emphasis on patient impact and translational relevance.

Affiliation

The HDSci Lab is an academic research group led by Dr. Tina Wang.
