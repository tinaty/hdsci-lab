Join the Lab

The HDSci Lab welcomes applications from motivated students and researchers interested in health data science, artificial intelligence, clinical informatics, and liver disease research.

We are particularly interested in individuals who are excited about interdisciplinary research at the intersection of healthcare, data science, and translational medicine.

Open Positions

  • PhD/DPhil students (health data science, statistics, AI)
  • Predoctoral and postdoctoral researchers
  • Visiting students and collaborators

What We Look For

  • Strong quantitative or computational background
  • Interest in AI and data science for healthcare
  • Experience in statistics, machine learning, or programming
  • Motivation to work in an interdisciplinary environment
  • Strong communication and collaborative skills

How to Apply

Please contact Dr Tina Wang with:

  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Brief description of research interests
  • Transcript (for students)
  • Relevant publications or project portfolio (if available)
