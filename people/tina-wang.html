About

Dr Tina Wang is the Principal Investigator of the Health Data Science & Intelligence Laboratory (HDSci Lab). Her research focuses on health data science, clinical artificial intelligence, translational informatics, and machine learning methods for liver disease and population health research.

The HDSci Lab develops computational methods and large-scale health data approaches to improve disease prediction, risk stratification, and precision medicine.

Research Interests

  • Clinical machine learning and artificial intelligence
  • Health data science and biomedical informatics
  • Liver disease and metabolic health
  • Electronic health record analytics
  • Predictive modeling and risk stratification
  • Translational data science
