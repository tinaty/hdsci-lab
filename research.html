Research

The HDSci Lab focuses on health data science and intelligence at the intersection of healthcare, data, and patients. Our work develops methods and applications that support better prediction, understanding, and decision-making in health.

Research Areas

  • Predictive modelling
  • Health literacy
  • Medical text analysis
  • Longitudinal cohort studies

Current Projects

  • NIHR Advanced Fellowship Project - Machine Learning for Chronic Hepatitis B
  • NIHR Health Informatics Collaborative (HIC) Viral Hepatitis and Liver Disease Theme
  • NHS TVS Secure Data Environment - Viral Hepatitis Driver Project

Methods

  • Machine learning and AI
  • NLP and large language models
  • Statistical modelling
